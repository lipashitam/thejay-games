How To Play

🎯 PURPOSE

Defeat as many enemies as possible with the right timing!

🎮 CONTROL

  • 💻 Desktop: Press the SPACEBAR or CLICK button
  • 📱 Mobile: TAP STRIKE button

⏱️ TIMING

  • 🟢 PERFECT: Green circle = +100 points
  • 🟡 GOOD: Yellow circle = +50 points
  • 🔴 MISS: Red circle = Loss of life

❤️ LIFE

You have 5 lives. Lose a life if you miss or the enemy escapes!

📈 DIFFICULTY

Enemies get faster with each new wave!
